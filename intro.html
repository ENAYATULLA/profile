About me

I'm a developer who loves reading novels. I'm originally from Bihar, a place with a rich culture, but I'm currently in Greater Noida, working on finishing my degree. Coding is like a puzzle to me, and I enjoy finding solutions to problems using technology. It's like a never-ending adventure where I get to be creative and logical at the same time. Apart from coding, I have a strong passion for novels. I love how they transport me to different places and times, and how I can feel like I'm part of the stories and characters. In the future, I hope to combine my love for technology and storytelling to create software that's not only functional but also meaningful to people's lives. It's like bringing together two worlds I'm deeply passionate about.
